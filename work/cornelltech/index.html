Cornell Tech

The launch of a new graduate school, the beginning of an era for NYC tech.

Cornell Tech is an innovative new graduate school in the heart of Manhattan. The web identity compliments the school’s forward-thinking goals and showcases it’s grand vision for New York City.

Building a school. Creating a brand.

We partnered with Cornell Tech to define their brand. The strategy included narrowing content scope and creating a clear, dynamic voice. Copy and wireframes were created in parallel to maximize the impact of our message.

Bold ideas. Bold design.

Punchy headlines, captivating visuals, and a New York City attitude capture the essence of Cornell Tech’s unique brand.

And this is only the beginning

The initial phases of Cornell Tech focused on building the brand and welcoming prospective students. As ground breaks on the massive Roosevelt Island construction project, the school’s identity, message, and impact will continue to grow well into 2017.
